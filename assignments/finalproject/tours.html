Tours Offered By Us

Beginning Tours

New to mountain biking and wondering where to start? Come with us on our mountain bike tours for beginners! We do different trails each week in the Bozeman mountains. All trails are moderately easy level riding. Meet us at our storefront at 7am with your mountain bike and gear and we will caravan to the trail of the week!
Beginner tours are led by our expert employee, Teddy Spokes, who will teach you the ins and outs of what to expect along the journey and also throw you some great pointers to improve your skill and style! Tours are $45. Payment will be expected at the store before we get started.

Expert Air Tours

These tours are for the bikers who are looking to take it to the next level! These tours take off every other Saturday starting at the storefront. Come with all of your essential gear. These will take place in the trails and bike parks in and around Bozeman. These trails will have no shortage of ramps, jumps, and tight corners. Come ready to get dirty!
Tours are led by the big boss himself, Jameson Spokes. We're out to have fun and learn from eachother, but Jameson is full of great tips to get your style on point. Tours are $60. Payment will be expected at the store before we get started.
