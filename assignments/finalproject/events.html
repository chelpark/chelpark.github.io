Cycle the Continental Divide

The Adventure of a Lifetime

Every year in June we take the best trip ever and bike the continental divide! It's not for the faint of heart! The route begins in Canada in Banff, AB. However, if you're not too worried about starting from the very beginning, many of us link up in Roosville, MT with the rest of our group about 250 miles into the journey. The journey ends in Antelope Wells, NM...a mere 2768 miles.


The Details:

  • June 6th - Start in Banff, AB
  • June 10th - Start in Roosville, MT
  • Fee - $400 paid by April 5th
  • Register below to be put on the list and will email you the pack list and also a suggested training schedule to get you ready for the big day!
